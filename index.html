Kagalarian Dictionary

This is the official Kagalarian dictionary created by Oneo64 for the worldbuilding project Starlattice!

Kagalarian is the main language spoken by citizens of the Kagalarian Empire. Although it doesn't belong to any specific language family, similar word construction patterns make it closest to Slavic (Russian specifically). Other than that, this language also takes inspiration from Norwegian, Hebrew, Arabic, Classical Latin, and sometimes, Basque.

This is the first conlang ever made by Oneo64. The original version was an attempt at a Germanic language, but failed spectacularly because it ended as an English relex and had non-Germanic word construction patterns. However, this version (website and spreadsheet versions) is an actual conlang with real grammatical rules, genders, cases, etc.

Kagalarian has a specific design in its word construction to make it sound powerful when spoken, while retaining its speakability. In the letter frequency chart, As and Os appear at the top two vowels in the lexicon. The most common noun ending is -on. However, words can also end in -ol, -al, -en, -o, etc. In casual speech, the language is usually spoken and written in the SVO word order. SOV is also common in rural areas and more traditional families.
