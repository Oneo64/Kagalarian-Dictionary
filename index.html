Kagalarian Dictionary

This is the official Kagalarian dictionary created by Oneo64 for the worldbuilding project Starlattice!

Kagalarian is the main language spoken by citizens of the Kagalarian Empire. Although it doesn't belong to any specific language family, the closest real-world family is Slavic. Other than that, this language also takes inspiration from Norwegian, Hebrew, Arabic, and even Classical Latin.

Kagalarian was the first conlang ever made by Oneo64, starting as an attempt at a Germanic language, yet failing and becoming a relex of the English language. However, in recent times, Kagalarian has been revamped into a fully-fledged conlang.

Kagalarian has a lot more Os and As than usual, for it is meant to sound powerful when spoken, while retaining its speakability. Many words in its lexicon end in -ol, -on, and -al. Kagalarian is usually spoken in the SOV word order, but SVO is sometimes accepted.
